Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions frequently asked about our services.

1. How long does it take to build a website?

+

Turnaround time varies. Landing Pages typically take 2-4 business days, while custom web applications take 1-3 weeks depending on complexity.

2. Are domain names included in the package prices?

+

Base package prices do not include a domain name. You can easily add it from the extras menu during checkout.

3. Are websites built by Ran Studio responsive and SEO friendly?

+

Absolutely. All our websites are strictly optimized for fast loading, include foundational SEO architecture, and are fully responsive across all devices.

4. What is the payment structure?

+

We require a 50% upfront deposit to commence development, with the remaining 50% due upon project validation and final approval.

5. Do I get access to an admin dashboard?

+

Yes, specifically for our E-Commerce and Custom System packages, we provide full access to a content management system (CMS).

6. Is there a warranty if the website encounters errors?

+

We provide a comprehensive bug-fix guarantee and dedicated technical support for 1 month post-launch.

7. Can I upgrade my package in the future?

+

Certainly! You can easily start with a Landing Page and upgrade features or add pages dynamically as your business scales.

8. What if I don't have a design prepared?

+

Not a problem. Our design team will craft the optimal UI/UX layout based entirely on your existing brand identity and color schemes.

9. What about annual renewal fees?

+

Annual fees only apply if you opt for our Domain add-on or third-party server hosting through us. All recurring costs are transparently stated upfront.

10. Do I need to prepare my own hosting?

+

It depends on your needs. If you have hosting, we can deploy there. Otherwise, our base systems utilize efficient cloud infrastructure with zero monthly hosting fees for static packages.

11. How long do revisions take?

+

Minor revisions (text adjustments, image swaps, color changes) are typically completed within 24 business hours.

12. Can I add custom features like a payment gateway?

+

Yes. Automated payment gateway integrations can be smoothly added via our Custom/Premium packages.

13. Do you provide copy and stock photos?

+

Ideally, base text and brand photos are provided by you. However, we can gladly assist with high-quality, royalty-free stock photography if needed.

14. Do I receive the website source code?

+

You retain full ownership. We will hand over the complete source code files upon project finalization if requested.

15. What do I need to get started?

+

Simply select a package, send us your business brief, make the 50% initial deposit, and we'll begin working on your project immediately!
